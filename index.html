How the Game Works

01
The Night Phase

The city sleeps. The Killer strikes, the Doctor heals, and the Detective investigates. All actions are secret until the sun rises.

02
The Day Phase

The town wakes up to find who was lost. Discussion begins. Use logic, intuition, and deception to find the killer among you.

03
The Trial

The town votes to eliminate one suspect. If the town eliminates all killers, they win. If the killer outnumbers the town, the shadows prevail.
